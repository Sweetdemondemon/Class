BBQ Bacon Cheeseburger

Get ready for a mouthwatering BBQ bacon cheeseburger! Packed with juicy cheese, crispy bacon, and bursting with flavor, this burger is a taste sensation you won't want to miss.

Ingredients

  • 1½ pound ground chuck 80%-85% lean
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoons chili powder
  • 2 teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon italian seasoning
  • 2 teaspoon Worchestire Sauce
  • ¼ BBQ sauce
  • Slices chedder cheese
  • Slice tomatoes
  • Cooked bacon slices
  • lettuce

How make the Bacon burgers

  1. in a large mixing bowl, gently mix ground beef with spices and Worchestire sauce. Be careful not to overmix Overmixingcan cause the burger to be tough instead of tender.
  2. From 4-6 large patties with the ground.
  3. Preheat the grill or griddle to at least 400° Fahrenhit. Once grill has preheate cook burgers 4-5 minues per side, or util the burgers reach 160° Fahrenheit
  4. Top the hamburger patty with a slice of chesse before removing them from the grill, for get flavor also toast the hamburgers buns and add your toppings.
